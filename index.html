About MITAOE CAMPUS

MIT Academy of Engineering is an Autonomous Engineering college Affiliated with the Savitribai Phule Pune University,
India and Accredited by NAAC with "A" Grade in 2014 & NBA Accredited. It was Established in the year 1999 and is approved and accredited by AICTE..
